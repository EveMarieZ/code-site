About Me

Hello! My name is Eve and I am a web developer who loves clean, elegant web design. I began as a design historian because I wanted to understand how design influences how we move through the world. I loved studying how design informed our past and decided to apply this knowledge to something contemporary and dynamic. Coding and web design has allowed me to create websites that are beautiful, functional, and unique.

I now use my history background to inform my web design practice, and strive to create websites for small businesses that reflect their history as well as the possibilities of their future. I love incorporating historic designs and textures into my work to give brands a refined, authoritative presence on the web. During the site build process, I work closely with my clients to ensure that their site is easy to navigate, encourages interaction, and effortlessly communicates their individual style to the world.

I hand-code all of my websites from scratch to develop an easy-to-use and engaging web experience. I will work with you to design an interface that will best suit your ideal clients and get your most important information on the web. I can also create custom email templates to keep your audience engaged with an email marketing campaign.

CSS will make your site stand out and your content shine! After coding the base structure of your site using HTML, I will bring your unique style to life using clean, well-organized CSS. I can translate your existing style to the new site, or I can create a color palette and design scheme that will effectively represent your brand identity and expertise.

Whether you have a design in mind or have already created your site in Photoshop, I can easily translate PSD files into a stunning new website. I can also incorporate accompanying design elements for your site including icons, logos, and illustrations to ensure your branding is consistent across the web and in printed media.
